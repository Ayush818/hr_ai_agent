Work Hours and Attendance Policy

Standard Work Hours:
The standard work week for full-time employees is 40 hours, typically Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with a one-hour unpaid lunch break.
Flexible arrangements may be possible with manager approval.

Attendance:
Employees are expected to be punctual and maintain regular attendance.
If you are going to be late or absent, please notify your manager as soon as possible, and no later than your scheduled start time.

Overtime:
Overtime work must be pre-approved by your manager.
Non-exempt employees will be compensated for approved overtime hours in accordance with applicable labor laws.
